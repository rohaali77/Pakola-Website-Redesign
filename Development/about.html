About Us

Pakola, a popular Pakistani beverage brand, was launched in 1950 by Haji Ali Mohammad. It is known for its distinctive green color and unique ice cream soda flavor. As Pakistan's first locally-produced soft drink, Pakola quickly became a cultural icon. Over the decades, it has expanded its product line to include various flavors and remains a beloved part of Pakistani heritage. .
